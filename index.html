About Us

Eagles is a prophetic Christ centered Ministry with the vision of reaching out the unreached,carrying along the vision of Christ and make it known to the Cities and nations(mathew 28:19-20). Eagles fellowship started in 2019 and is now celebrating 4years of Ministry with Apostle Thamani Israel with other spiritual leaders as place for fellow believers to congress and worship together. Eagles ministry is actively involved to high school mission,door to door envangelism and street mission and crusade.The recent and active works to prepare the body of Christ for the Second coming of the Messiah.
